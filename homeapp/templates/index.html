About Us

"MET'S Polytechnic College is one of the Best Government approved Self-financing Polytechnic College in Kerala, which offers a wide range of 3-Year Diploma Courses with IBM Certification.
The Institution is Approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and Affiliated to State Board of Technical Education (SBTE), Kerala. The successful completion of these courses helps the Students to start his or her profession, either through a job at a well-reputed Government/Private Company or through Startups or by joining for Higher Studies like BTech, BVoc etc. The 3-Year Diploma Courses offered @ METS are: 1. Mechanical Engineering 2. Electrical & Electronics Engineering 3. Computer Engineering 4. Architecture 5. Commercial Practice
